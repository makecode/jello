11 Hidden Snapchat Tricks and Tips You Ought to Know

1. Take discreet screenshot without being noticed

Sometimes we’d like to take a screenshot without being noticed to fool our friends for fun afterward. Just follow the steps below:

  • Turn on airplane mode.
  • Take the screenshot and quickly log out of Snapchat.
  • Turn off airplane mode and log in your account again.
2. Check on who’s following you and who has unfollowed you on Snapchat

If you are curious about whether someone follows you or not, you may actually find the answer.

  • Firstly, you need to follow that person you want to check on.
  • Go to “Add Friends”, and then tap on “Add by Username”.
  • After typing in that person’s username, hold your finger on it.
  • It means that the person is following you if you can see his or her Snapchat score.
  • By following the same procedure, you may find out who has unfollowed you.
3. Type in unlimited texts
  • Go to your phone’s notes and create a blank note page
  • Tap on the return button several times to create some blank lines.
  • Hold finger on the note page and choose “Select All” and “Copy.”
  • Go back to Snapchat, hold finger on the text field and choose “Paste.”
  • Then you will see a much bigger text space.
4. Find extra color besides the color picker

If you take a close look at the rainbow tool, you will find no sign of black or white pencil options. There are a few steps needed to search them out.

  • Hold onto the color picker and drag your finger anywhere on the screen, you may find numerous color pencil shown on the top right side.
  • Drag your finger to the top-left side; you will see the white pencil.
  • Drag your finger to the downright side; you will see the black pencil.
  • If you want a lighter version of the same color, just hold your finger down, and begin to drag it to the left slowly. You’ll notice that the shade begins to get lighter; Once you notice the color has changed, you can drag your finger directly straight down. And you will get a darker version of the same color.”
